# Organizations

Organizations are the top-level tenant in LearnHouse. Every piece of content, every user, and every setting is scoped to an organization.

## What an Organization Contains

Each organization has its own:

- **Courses and collections** — All educational content is owned by the organization.
- **Users** — Members belong to an organization and are assigned roles within it.
- **Settings** — Branding, AI configuration, signup rules, and more are configured per-organization.
