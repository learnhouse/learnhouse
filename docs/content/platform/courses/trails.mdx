# Trails

Trails let students track their progress through courses, providing a clear view of what they have completed and what is left.

<br/>
<img style={{borderRadius:10}} quality={100} src="/img/pages/trails/trails.png" alt="Trails overview" width={800} height={500} />

## How Trails Work

When a student starts a course, a Trail is automatically created for them. As they work through activities, their progress is recorded — including activity completions and quiz submissions.

Students can view their Trail at any time to see:

- Which activities they have completed
- Quiz results and scores
- Overall course progression

This encourages self-directed learning and helps students stay on track.
