# Offers & Pricing

Offers are the products you sell to students. Each offer defines what students get, how much they pay, and whether the payment is recurring or one-time.

## Offer Types

- **Subscription** — Recurring payments that grant access as long as the subscription is active.
- **One-time purchase** — A single payment that grants permanent access.

## Pricing Options

- **Fixed price** — Set a specific amount in your chosen currency.
- **Customer choice** — Let students pay what they want (pay-what-you-want model).

## Offer Details

Each offer includes:

- **Name & description** — What the student is buying.
- **Benefits** — A list of what's included in the offer.
- **Amount & currency** — The price (e.g., $49 USD).
- **Public listing** — Whether the offer appears on your public storefront.
- **Linked resources** — Courses or groups that the offer grants access to.

## Public Storefront

Offers marked as public are displayed on your organization's storefront page. Each listing shows the offer details, included courses, and a purchase button that launches the Stripe checkout flow.
