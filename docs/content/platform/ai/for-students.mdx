# AI for Students

Students can interact with the LearnHouse AI assistant while working through course content. The AI acts as an on-demand tutor that understands the material being studied.

## Context-Aware Chat

When a student opens the AI chat from within a course or activity, the assistant automatically has context about:

- The current activity content
- The broader course material
- The chapter structure and related topics

This allows students to ask questions and receive answers that are directly relevant to what they are studying, rather than generic responses.

## Example Use Cases

- **Clarifying concepts** — Ask the AI to explain a topic from the current lesson in different terms.
- **Summarizing content** — Request a summary of a long activity or chapter.
- **Practice questions** — Ask the AI to generate practice questions based on the current material.
- **Deeper exploration** — Explore related topics that build on the course content.
