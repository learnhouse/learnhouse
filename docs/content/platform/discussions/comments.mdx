# Comments & Reactions

Comments and reactions are the primary ways users engage with discussion threads.

## Comments

Any member of the organization can reply to a discussion with a comment. Comments support text content and are displayed in chronological order within the thread.

### Voting on Comments

Comments can be upvoted by other users. This helps surface the most helpful or relevant replies within a discussion. Vote counts are displayed alongside each comment.

## Reactions

Reactions let users respond to discussions quickly with emoji reactions. Multiple users can add the same reaction, and the count is displayed on the discussion.

## Moderation

Teachers and admins have additional controls over discussions:

- **Pin** a discussion to keep it at the top of the list.
- **Lock** a discussion to prevent further comments.
- **Delete** comments or discussions that violate guidelines.
