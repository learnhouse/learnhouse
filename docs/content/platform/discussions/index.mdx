# Discussions

Discussions provide a community space within your organization where students and teachers can ask questions, share ideas, and collaborate outside of course content.

## How Discussions Work

Discussions are organized within communities tied to your organization. Users can create threads, reply with comments, vote on posts and comments, and react with emojis.

<Callout>
Discussions must be enabled in your [Organization Settings](/platform/organizations/settings) to be available.
</Callout>

## Key Features

- **Threaded conversations** — Create discussion topics and reply with comments for focused conversations.
- **Voting** — Upvote discussions and comments to surface the most helpful content.
- **Reactions** — React to discussions with emojis to provide quick feedback.
- **Pinning & locking** — Teachers and admins can pin important discussions or lock threads that have been resolved.
- **Labels** — Categorize discussions with labels for easy filtering and discovery.
